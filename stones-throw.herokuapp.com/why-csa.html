Why CSA?

We prefer to market our produce through the CSA model because it offers some incredible advantages for both you, our consumers, and us as farmers.

Advantages for the Consumer

  • Every week you get super fresh, flavorful, vitamin-rich food.
  • Meet and form a relationship with the farmers who grow your food!
  • Opportunities to visit the farm and learn how the food is grown.

Advantages for the Farmers

  • Meet and form relationships with the people who eat the food we grow.
  • Help us stabilize the summer cash flow early in the season.
  • Can spend time marketing the food before the long planting, weeding, and harvesting days begin.
