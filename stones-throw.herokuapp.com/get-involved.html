Get Involved

This season, there will be lots of ways to get involved with Stone's Throw. We couldn't do what we do without support from our community, so thanks in advance for all your help!

CSA Membership

The most common way to get involved with Stone's Throw is to become a CSA member. As a member of our farm, you'll enjoy fresh veggies throughout the season, get a chance to pick your own cherry tomatoes and herbs on our urban farm plots, build community with other members are our potlucks, and take an active role in supporting urban agriculture.

Volunteer Opportunities

We will have a number of volunteer opportunities throughout the growing season. If you would like to be added to our volunteer email list so that you can receive updates about work days and other opportunities, please send us an email or fill out our contact form. Emails will be forwarded to Eric, our volunteer coordinator.

Employment

We are working with the Step Up Achieve program in 2013 to employ two high-school aged youth part time during the summer. If you know of youth who might be interested in working with us, please have them contact the Step Up Program.

Work Share

We offer a limited number of work shares, and all our spots for 2013 are filled. A work share entails 5-6 hours a week of work on the farm in exchange for a weekly share of vegetables. A consistent commitment over a 20-week period is required. Check back with us next fall and winter if you are interested in a work share in 2014.

Donation

As a new business committed to serving our community, there's always a need for funds. If you are interested in donating, please email us.

Land

Secure land tenure if one of our biggest challenges as urban farmers. If you own or have information on land that could be used for farming, please let us know! Here are our criteria:

  • Land must be at least a full lot. Side yards are rarely large enough to make it worth it for us to use.
  • We can use any size parcel between 1/10th of an acre and 2 acres.
  • The farther from our hubs (South Minneapolis and Frogtown) the land is, the larger the parcel must be to be useful for us.
  • Water must be available on-site (we reimburse for water usage).
  • A minimum of 3 years land tenure, preferably more long-term.
