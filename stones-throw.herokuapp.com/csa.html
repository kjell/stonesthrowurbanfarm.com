Stone's Throw CSA Program

Stone's Throw Urban Farm offers our produce through a Community Supported Agriculture (CSA) program. The CSA model is a way to build a stronger relationship between you and your food by becoming a member of our farm. Members purchase a "share" of produce before the season begins, then receive a portion of our freshest and most seasonal vegetables each week of the growing season. Our CSA members are our valued customers and neighbors; we strive to grow you the best food we can.

  • We offer both full and half shares: a full share contains 10-15 pounds of produce per week, a half 5-7 lbs.
  • Your box will be filled with fresh, seasonal vegetables each week, with a mix of staples and exciting varieties.
  • The season will run for 20 weeks, from early June through late October.
  • Our shares are an affordable way to eat healthy—an average share costs about $25/week, or roughly $2/lb!
  • We accept applications on a first come, first serve basis—so hurry to secure a share in our harvest!

What's in a Share?

A Stone's Throw CSA will help you fill your home with fresh, seasonal produce. Our shares offer a diverse array of veggies; farm-fresh favorites like carrots, tomatoes, cucumbers, and potatoes share space with unsung heroes like Japanese salad turnips, kohlrabi, garlic scapes, and broccoli raab. Here are a few details about our share:

The season will run for approximately 20 weeks, from June through October. Exact start and end dates are subject to the weather, and will be announced in a timely fashion.

Each week's share will be accompanied by a newsletter written by your farmers, containing notes from the week, recipes, and photos.

Access to a member recipe sharing board- learn new tips and teach others how to cook even the least popular veggies.

On-farm pick-up options at our beautiful urban farm sites. See the changes of the season, interact with your farmer, see where your food grows- without ever leaving the city!

U-pick options for tasty items like cherry tomatoes, green beans, peas, flowers, and herbs.

Community events such as potlucks, storytelling, harvest party, and more.

Optional add-ons: in 2013 we hope to offer CSA add-ons from other local producers, such as bread, honey, and mushrooms.

What do I get each week?

Each week's array of veggies will change with the season and availability. To give an idea of what a typical share looks like, take a look at a typical, early, mid, and late season's spread:

June: 1⁄2 lb. salad mix, 1⁄2 lb. arugula, 1⁄2 lb spinach, 3 kohlrabi, handful of garlic scapes, large bunch kale or chard, bunch of salad turnips, bunch of radishes, u-pick peas and herbs

August: 2 lbs. beets, 1 lb carrots, 4 small eggplant, 2 summer squash, 2 onions, 2 sweet peppers, 1 lb potatoes, 3 lb. tomatoes, 4 lb. cucumbers, handful hot peppers, u-pick cherry tomatoes, green beans, flowers, herbs

October: 1 lb. beets, 2 lb. carrots, 3 lb. potatoes, large bunch kale or chard, 2 large onions, butternut squash, acorn squash, 2 purple-top turnips, bunch salad turnips, 1⁄2 lb salad mix or spinach, 2 heads garlic

With the passage of seasons, good weather, and hard work, we hope to bring you another bounteous season in 2013!

Share Options

Stone's Throw shares are offered on a sliding scale. We hope that this flexibility in pricing allows members of differing financial circumstances to join with us for the season. Sliding scale is self-selecting: simply choose what you feel able to pay, within the given range.

Full Share: 3⁄4 bushel, $500-$675. 10-15 lbs of produce, depending on season. This share should feed 3-4 moderate veggie eaters, or 1-2 avid veggie lovers.

Half Share: 1⁄2 bushel, $300-$400. 5-7 lbs of produce, depending on season. This share should feed 1-2 moderate veggie eaters.

Work Share: We are offering a limited number (1-2) of work shares in the 2013 season. You'll receive a full share of produce in exchange for 5 hours/week of work.

Pick-up Locations

St. Paul: 391 Maryland Avenue, North End Urban Farm. Tuesday 3:30-6:30 PM.
South Minneapolis: 2820 15th Avenue S. Tuesday 3:30-6:30 PM

* St. Paul and South Minneapolis pick-up locations are working farm sites. Shares are not boxed, but are distributed in a semi-self serve fashion similar to a market stand. Items in the share are pre-determined, but a small amount of choice is sometimes possible. U-pick on site. Please bring your own bag or box.*

Mill City Market: Downtown Minneapolis, between the Guthrie and the Mill City Museum. Saturday 8 AM-1 PM.

* Mill City Market shares are available for pick-up at our market stand. Shares are boxed, and do not include on-site u-pick. Mill City members can either u-pick at either farm pick-up site during the week, or purchase the items pre-picked at a discounted price. *

Honey Shares

A sweet addition to your Stone's Throw CSA - honey from The Beez Kneez! Each month from June-October, you'll receive a jar of honey along with your veggie share. This local, raw honey is delivered by bike and will bring a sweet twist to your CSA!

Small honey share: 1 pint (1.12#) per month for $43.

Large honey share: 3# container per month for $108.
