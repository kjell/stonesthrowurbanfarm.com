Stone's Throw Urban Farm is redefining local, sustainable food in the Twin Cities.

We convert vacant lots in St. Paul and Minneapolis into beautiful, productive micro-farms and grow food for a CSA, the Mill City Farmers Market, and various local wholesale accounts. Stone's Throw is working hard to strengthen our local food system.

Our farm is run by a partnership of six farmers, with help from neighbors and volunteers. As an agro-ecologically minded small business rooted in community values, we strive to transform unused urban spaces into vibrant farmland. We farm on over 16 formerly vacant lots in both South Minneapolis and the Frogtown neighborhood of St. Paul. The veggies we harvest are sold through a Community Supported Agriculture (CSA) program, at Mill City Farmers' Market, and to several local restaurants.

2013 CSA season is upon us!

Our pickups begin Tuesday, June 11th, Saturday, June 15th for Mill City Members.

St. Paul: 391 Maryland Avenue, North End Urban Farm. Tuesday 3:30-6:30 PM.
South Minneapolis: 2820 15th Avenue S. Tuesday 3:30-6:30 PM

* St. Paul and South Minneapolis pick-up locations are working farm sites. Shares are not boxed, but are distributed in a semi-self serve fashion similar to a market stand. Items in the share are pre-determined, but a small amount of choice is sometimes possible. U-pick on site. Please bring your own bag or box.*

Mill City Market: Downtown Minneapolis, between the Guthrie and the Mill City Museum. Saturday 8 AM-1 PM.

* Mill City Market shares are available for pick-up at our market stand. Shares are boxed, and do not include on-site u-pick. Mill City members can either u-pick at either farm pick-up site during the week, or purchase the items pre-picked at a discounted price. *
